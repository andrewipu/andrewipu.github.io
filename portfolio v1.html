About Me

Since I was little, I have always been facinated by computers. Probably because I spent too much time admiring my older siblings assemble personal computers from scratch.


This was the spark that ignited my love for computers & the technologies behind them. When I was 12, I assembled my very own (and first!) personal computer from scratch purely from hand-me-down components.


4 years later I dipped my toes into the world of mobile programming by attempting to make an Android app and in the midst of tutorial hell and lack of perseverance, I gave up shortly after. This hindered any positive future relations with programming as the initial failure had brought forth new anxiety.


However, thanks to my exposure whilst pursuing a computer science degree as well as FreeCodeCamp, I am now embracing my new-found love for programming. My goal/dream is to collaborate with others to make wonderful products that can be used by everyone.


I am a recent graduate in Applied Computer Technology at the United States International University - Africa (USIU-A). This portfolio is as a result of my hard work & resilience over the past several months and I look forward to carrying out future improvements here as I continually improve upon my software development skills.
